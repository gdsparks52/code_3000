## Personal Ethics Statement
I believe that ethical technology development requires balancing innovation with responsibility. Drawing from transparency, accountability, and human autonomy, I will commit to:

- Designing systems that prioritize user privacy and informed consent
- Acknowledging potential harms and unintended consequences in my work
- Advocating for a design that considers all stakeholder perspectives
- Continuously learning about the potential ethical implications of emerging technologies
- Speaking up when I encounter practices that compromise user welfare, fairness, or security

Technology shapes society, and data scientists have a duty to consider the broader impact of our choices and usage of your information.
